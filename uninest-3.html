Your journey on UniNest

Two paths. You choose
where to start.

Not sure how it all works? Pick your situation below and we'll walk you through exactly what happens — step by step.

1
📝
Sign up & build your profile
Enter your name, university, course, year, and monthly budget. This takes 2 minutes. Your budget and university are used to filter everything you see — so you only see rooms that actually fit you.
Budget filter University filter Gender preference
↓
2
🔍
Browse & shortlist rooms
Search rooms near your campus. Filter by price, furnishing, gender preference, distance, and amenities. Each listing shows the number of open slots. You pick rooms you like — nothing is assigned to you.
You choose the room See open slots Safety score shown
↓
3
✍️
Take the compatibility quiz
Answer 10 quick questions about your sleep schedule, cleanliness, noise level, guests, and study habits. This only takes 3 minutes. Your answers build your lifestyle profile — used to match you with other students interested in the same room.
10 questions ~3 minutes 40+ factors analysed
↓
4
🤝
See who else wants that room
UniNest shows you other verified students who have enquired about or shortlisted the same room. It then runs your profiles through the compatibility algorithm and gives each person a match score — like 92%, 88%, 74%. You are not randomly assigned. You see the scores and decide who to message.
💡 Example: Room 14B has 2 open slots. You shortlisted it. So did Amina (92% match) and James (74% match). You see both, with scores explained. You choose who to reach out to.
↓
5
💬
Message your match
Chat inside UniNest, ask questions, get a feel for each other. Once you're both happy, you both enquire on the room together as a pair. The landlord sees a confirmed pair and is more likely to say yes immediately.
↓
✓
🏡
Arrange viewing & move in
Request a viewing directly from the listing. Confirm the room, pay the deposit, and move in. The whole process — from signup to confirmed room — typically takes 3 to 7 days.
✓ Viewing via UniNest ✓ Deposit direct to landlord
1
📝
Sign up & build your profile
Same first step — enter your university, budget, course, and gender preference. Even if you don't have a room in mind yet, your profile tells UniNest what kind of person you are and what you need.
↓
2
✍️
Take the compatibility quiz first
Answer the 10-question lifestyle quiz before you even look at rooms. This is the core of the matching — sleep time, cleanliness, noise, guests, study habits. Your answers are your "compatibility fingerprint."
Do you smoke? Sleep schedule? How clean? (1–10) Do you host guests?
↓
3
🎯
See your top roommate matches
UniNest instantly shows you verified students from your university (or nearby) who are also looking for accommodation, ranked by compatibility. A 92% match means you aligned on almost everything. A 74% match might have one lifestyle conflict flagged clearly so you can decide.
💡 Matches are explained: "✓ Same sleep schedule ✓ Both non-smokers ✓ Same cleanliness level ⚠ She hosts guests on weekends." You see the full picture before you message anyone.
↓
4
💬
Connect with your match
Message your top match inside UniNest. Talk about what area you want, what budget you're both working with, and what type of room you want. Once you're comfortable with each other, you move to the next step together.
↓
5
🔍
Search for a room together
Now you browse listings as a confirmed pair. You can filter rooms that have 2 open slots, are near your shared university, and fit both your budgets. You already like each other — now you just need the right room.
Filter: 2 slots available Combined budget Shared uni area
↓
✓
🏡
Enquire together & move in
You both send an enquiry to the landlord as a pair — this is a strong signal that the slots will be filled immediately. Arrange a joint viewing, confirm, and move in with someone you actually chose and like.
❓
Is the room assigned to me?
No. You browse and choose every room yourself, filtered by your budget and preferences. Nothing is random or automatic.
❓
Is the roommate assigned to me?
No. You see a ranked list of compatible students with scores and reasons. You choose who to message — no one is forced on you.
❓
What if I want to live alone?
Filter for studio or single-occupancy rooms. You don't need to use the roommate matching feature at all — it's optional.
❓
Do I pay through UniNest?
Rent and deposits are paid directly to the landlord. UniNest only charges KES 499/month for Premium — everything else is free.
